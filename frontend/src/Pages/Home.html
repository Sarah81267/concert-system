Frequently Asked Questions

Can I book tickets for someone else?

Yes, during the checkout process, you can enter the recipient’s details to transfer the tickets to them.

How will I receive my tickets?

Tickets are delivered digitally to your registered email. You can also access them through your account on our website.

Can I get a physical copy of my ticket?

We primarily offer digital tickets. However, some events may allow physical ticket delivery for an additional fee.

What should I do if I don’t receive my ticket?

Check your spam/junk email folder. If you still can’t find it, contact our support team for assistance.
